Project Information

  • Project Title: Chatbot Enabled Fitness Center Web App
  • Project Description:

    About the company: ChatbotIO specializes in developing state-of-the-art AI chatbots designed to understand and cater to specific user interests. Our solutions provide detailed instructions and recommendations, enhancing user experience and ensuring client satisfaction.

    About the project: This initiative focuses on the development and implementation of an AI-driven analysis of client questionnaire results. The goal is to offer a more personalized service, enhancing user satisfaction and increasing the likelihood of users achieving their fitness goals.

  • Problem/Opportunity Assessment:
    • Vague questions: Many platforms use generic questions to assess clients' fitness interests and goals, leading to less accurate user profiling.
    • Lack of Interactive Assistance: Without an interactive helper, potential or current clients may feel lost or unsupported during their fitness journey.
    • One-size-fits-all Recommendations: Users often receive generic advice, which doesn't cater to their unique needs, leading to decreased motivation and adherence.
  • Desired Project Outcomes/Requirements:
    • Detailed questions that are applicable to multiple types of usersâ€™ requirements
    • Develop questions that accurately gauge potential users' fitness goals and unique requirements, ensuring a more personalized user experience.
    • Interactive assistant to answer user questions/inquiries
    • Chat bot to answer the users' questions
    • Detailed and personalized guidance for the user to achieve fitness goals
    • Collecting the results from the questionnaire and give the user personalized direction and guidance on how to achieve their fitness goals
    • Feedback Mechanism: Establish a system where users can provide feedback on their plans, allowing for continuous improvement and refinement based on real-world experiences.
  • Key Deliverables to be produced by students:
    • Gym website: Design a user-friendly platform where users can seamlessly access the questionnaire and interact with the chatbot.
    • Comprehensive Questionnaire: Curate a set of questions that delve deep into users' fitness goals and unique challenges.
    • AI Analysis Module: Develop and integrate an AI algorithm capable of analyzing questionnaire results and generating personalized fitness guidance.
