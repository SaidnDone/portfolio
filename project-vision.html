1. Introduction

We create a chatbot to analyze and give recommendations on potential and current clients’ fitness and/or weight-loss goals.

Purpose

To give clients recommendations and instructions on how to achieve their fitness and weight-loss goals.

Scope

A website that has a questionnaire for users to fill out and give an analysis based on their input.

In Scope
  • Creation of gym website
  • Questionnaire
  • AI implementation to analyze questionnaire
Out of Scope
  • Sourcing gym equipment
  • Advertising

Definitions, Acronyms, and Abbreviations

Chatbot, AI, fitness, weight-loss, plan, gym

2. Positioning

Business Opportunity

By leveraging AI-driven analysis of user questionnaire results, there's an opportunity to attract more potential clients to the fitness center. Personalized recommendations can significantly enhance user satisfaction and commitment.

Problem Statement

The Problem of Individuals keen on health and fitness often struggle due to a lack of proper guidance or not witnessing desired results.
Affects Potential clients of the fitness center
The Impact of Which Is With proper guidance, many can embark on a more structured and effective fitness journey.
A Successful Solution Would Be An AI-driven analysis of user-filled questionnaires, providing clear instructions tailored to individual fitness goals.

Product Position Statement

  • For: People interested in fitness and weight-loss
  • Who: Individuals keen on fitness and weight-loss who lack proper guidance, the ChatBotIO website, equipped with an AI-driven chatbot, will offer personalized instructions to achieve their fitness goals.
  • The website with chatbot: Will give people clear instructions on how to achieve their fitness goals
  • Unlike: Unlike other gyms that primarily showcase pricing and locations, our platform provides tailored advice based on individual user responses.
  • Our product: After filling out the questionnaire, an AI will analyze each user’s results and give guidance on how to achieve their fitness goals.

7. Constraints

  • 1. The development team must adhere to the allocated budget.
  • 2. The system should be compatible with common web browsers and mobile devices.
  • 3. Compliance with data privacy regulations, such as GDPR or HIPAA, must be maintained.
  • 4. System development should be completed within the specified timeframe.
  • 5. Availability of third-party APIs for personalized workout plans and nutritional recommendations may be a constraint.
